<objective>
Wire the ADAPT-04 weekly adaptation check to a real caller. Today `POST /adaptations/check` has zero callers (v1.0 milestone audit); this plan adds a client-initiated lazy trigger (D-01/D-02) that fires at most once per 7 days (D-03), fire-and-forget (D-04), and — critically — does NOT advance the throttle timestamp on failure (D-05) so a transient network/401 blip cannot silently disable the check for a full week.

Purpose: ADAPT-04 exists to catch accumulating fatigue no single upload triggers; without a caller it never runs. The mount-once `AppLayout` is the correct single integration point (13-RESEARCH.md `## AppLayout Mount Mechanics`, Pitfall 2) so every entry point (Today, Coach, Agenda, Progress) covers the check, not just one screen.
Output: `useAdaptationCheck` hook + its throttle/failure test + a one-line call in `AppLayout`.
</objective>
