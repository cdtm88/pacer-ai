<objective>
Close the two open findings from 03-VERIFICATION.md (status: gaps_found, score 22/23) without touching plans 03-01..03-05.

- CR-01 (BLOCKER): `_build_sessions` (backend/sports_science/plan.py) cycles `preferred_days` via modulo when `len(preferred_days) < n_sessions`, producing two distinct sessions with the same (week, day) in every week whenever the weekly session count is not an exact multiple of the preferred-days count (e.g. 2 preferred days, 4 sessions/week — an ordinary onboarding answer). `_resolve_all_scheduled_dates` (backend/agent/tools.py) then fails to de-collide them because its first pass assigns every non-rolled session its naive date and adds it to `used` WITHOUT checking `used` for a pre-existing collision — its collision handling only special-cases the week-1-roll-forward scenario. Result: 8 of 16 sessions in a 4-week plan share a scheduled_date, permanently breaking rides.py's date-keyed session match and causing adaptations.detect_signals to perpetually mis-flag ridden sessions as "missed" (corrupts FIT-04, FIT-05, ADAPT-01). Fix in the resolver (the smaller, more targeted change that also catches every collision source, verified to preserve both existing scheduling tests) plus a regression test with `preferred_days` shorter than `n_sessions`.

- WR-06 (recommended, non-blocking): `sse_generator` (backend/routes/_sse.py) persists accumulated assistant text to the `messages` table whenever the generator loop exits without a raised exception. But `run_turn`'s abnormal paths (`max_tool_turns`, `unexpected_stop`, `max_retries`) yield an `error` event and then `return` normally (a normal generator exit, not an exception), so partial/truncated text is persisted as if the turn completed successfully — a reliability gap in the TRANSP-01 chat-transparency channel. Fix by tracking the terminal event type and appending to `assistant_sink` only when the turn ended on `done`.

Purpose: Restore the "no two sessions share a scheduled_date" invariant that success criteria 3 and 4 depend on, and make the transparency-channel persistence honor its own documented "nothing is appended on the error path" contract.
Output: Collision-aware date resolution, done-gated assistant persistence, and three regression tests that lock both fixes.
</objective>
