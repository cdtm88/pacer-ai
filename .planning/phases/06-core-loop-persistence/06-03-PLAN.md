<objective>
Replace the broken single-EWMA-step-per-upload PMC model with a recompute-from-scratch day-series builder. Today `update_pmc` runs once per upload keyed by `date.today()`: CTL/ATL never decay on rest days, same-day second uploads double-step and overwrite `tss`, and retroactive uploads corrupt the series. This new module rebuilds the entire canonical daily-TSS series (grouped and summed by `ride_date`) from the user's earliest ride to today, walks every calendar day through the existing pure `update_pmc`, and bulk-upserts the resulting `pmc_history` rows. `sports_science/pmc.py` stays pure and untouched (locked invariant). This module is imported and called inline by the ride pipeline in plan 06-05.

Phase 6 has no new requirement IDs; FIT-04 and TOOL-05 are the existing requirements this plan makes correct.

Purpose: PMC must be correct across gap days, same-day rides, and re-uploads.
Output: `backend/pmc_recompute.py::recompute_pmc_for_user` plus `tests/test_pmc_recompute.py`.
Note (orchestrator decision): PMC recompute is triggered ONLY from ride upload this phase; do not add a manual/admin recompute endpoint.
</objective>
