<objective>
Correct the mislabeled HR zone constants and add the LTHR-estimation tool the onboarding flow needs.

Defect #6 (D-06): `HR_ZONE_BOUNDARIES` are Friel-style (81/90/94/100% of LTHR) but the docstring and tool description claim "Coggan/Allen" (~68/83/94/105%). The mislabeled Zone 2 ceiling (90%) is also too hot for a returning, back-flagged beginner. Correcting the constant to the true Coggan/Allen boundaries makes the methodology honest AND lowers the Zone 2 ceiling to 83% — one fix, both roadmap concerns.

D-05 (max-HR branch): the onboarding LTHR question (Plan 07) needs an authoritative, methodology-tagged way to convert a user-reported max HR into an LTHR estimate. This plan builds and registers that tool now (interface-first) so Plan 07 only has to wire the prompt. Registering it here also keeps `backend/agent/tools.py` owned by a single Wave-1 plan.

Purpose: TOOL-02 amendment (D-06) + the estimator half of ONBD-05 (D-05).
Output: corrected constants, a new registered `estimate_lthr_from_max_hr` tool, extended zone tests.
</objective>
